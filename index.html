Before you start

🚫NO WATER — only dilute with Fixativ
🧪Test patch first — let it dry fully
🔄Stir before pouring — re-stir every 10–15 min
🔩Mix with a drill mixer only — do not mix by hand. Use a mechanical paddle/drill attachment for at least 2 minutes
🖌️Wet edge — don't stop mid-wall
📦Only mix what you need for the day — don't batch ahead
🧱Mineral substrates only — bare concrete, cement render, masonry. Not for plasterboard, timber, metal or painted surfaces
✂️Apply in cross-hatch pattern — work in alternating directions for even coverage
🌡️Temperature 5°C – 30°C — air and substrate. Do not apply in direct sunlight, frost, rain or strong wind
⏳Drying time ~12 hrs — wait at least 12 hours between coats. Allow longer in cold or humid conditions
💧Pre-dampen porous surfaces — lightly mist with water before applying to very absorbent concrete
